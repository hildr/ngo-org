Switch off the oppression against women

The oppression of women is the biggest moral problem of the 21st century. Statistical 20 to 50 percent of women experience oppression at some point of their life. It can be partner violence, physical and sexual abuse, exploitation, honor killing, early marriage, prohibition on education and other. But together we can stop that, together we can switch the oppression off!
